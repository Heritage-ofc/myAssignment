The Beginner’s Guide to Backend Development

Backend development (also stylized as back-end or back end development) is the skill that powers the web.

Yet it does it modestly, without fanfare—allowing people to browse their favorite sites without even knowing about all the work put in by the backend developer or team. So to help dispel this mystery… what is backend development?

How do backend technologies work? What job does a back-end developer perform? What are some back end developer languages and backend tools? How do you learn backend development? In this special backend 101 guest post, Krystal Tolani from GoSkills writes about the various types of backend coding languages, what you can do with them as a backend developer, companies that use backend development, and (the money question!)

what salaries you can earn by specializing in certain backend skills.
